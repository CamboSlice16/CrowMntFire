Dan Wood

Scott Russell

Chief Russell began his career as a firefighter in May of 1992. In 1994, he was promoted to Lieutenant, and in 2000, he was promoted to Battalion Chief. In August of 2016, he was promoted to Fire Chief. Chief Russell holds a Bachelor of Science in Natural Science/Biology from Arkansas Tech University. He is currently enrolled in the Executive Fire Officer Program at the National Fire Academy.
